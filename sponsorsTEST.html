SUPPORT US!

We are a group of progressive and creative makers striving to expand our skills and bring positive change to our community in Kamloops. By supporting us in achieving our goals, you are helping to unleash an incredible potential: the power to join hands and improve our lives by sharing our skills. Kamloops Makespace is the hub that connects all the makers in our community, including creative individuals such as artists and technology enthiusiasts as well as entrepreneurs and innovative businesses.

We rely on generous support of sponsors to be able to provide the tools and the creative space to the community. We would like to thank our generous donors over the years.
